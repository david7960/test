SCTM Calculator V1.0

This study aimed to develop and validate a simplified CT model (SCTM) based on the Bosniak classification (v2019) for predicting the malignancy risk of cystic renal masses (CRMs).

The interactive SCTM is easy to use. Simply input the two characteristics shown below, and the malignancy risk of cystic renal masses (CRMs) will be presented.

SCTM does not collect or store any individual data.
